Vote

Voting is easy. We only ask you 2 questions: How likely you feel you are to succeed at your job, and how likely you feel your company is going to succeed as a whole. You display your preferences visually on a scatterplot, and after voting you get to see your colleagues' results as well.

Earn Rewards

Earn rewards points every time you vote. Earn 10 points if your last vote was within 7 days, and 5 points if your last vote date was greater than 7 days from the day your vote. Redeem your reward points with our affiliates.
